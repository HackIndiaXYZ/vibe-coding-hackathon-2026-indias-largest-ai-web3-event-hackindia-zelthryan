## 🎯 Problem
Students stuff their resumes with clichés. Friends give fake praise. Placement cells are overwhelmed.  
Real feedback only comes after rejection — too late.

## 💡 Solution
AI that actually reads your resume and **roasts it** — pointing out every weak phrase, missing metric, and cringe buzzword. Then tells you exactly how to fix it.

---

## 🧠 How It Works
1. Paste your resume text  
2. AI roasts it with savage humour  
3. Get a Burn Score + 3 actionable fixes  
4. Copy and share your roast on LinkedIn
